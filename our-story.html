Two Lives Become One

Ever since they met in 2015, Dan & Bridget have matured, grown, and overcome challenges with each other. Each step in their adventures together has led to knowing and loving each other more deeply and fully.


They are ready to leap into the next step of life together, committing themselves to a life of love, happiness, and faith together forever. We would love to have as many friends and family members as possible join us in this joyous and exciting celebration!

October 01, 2015

We both were very involved with Young Life during our high school years, and we ran across each other on multiple occasions, the first time being at Lake Champion summer camp where Dan was notorious for "photo-bombing" some of Bridget's photos by being a goofball with a mutual friend, Mason. We both ended up becoming Wyld Life leaders, the middle school version of Young Life, in our respective towns of Clarence and Tonawanda. Bridget and Mason came back to sit in and watch Dan help lead in Tonawanda a few times, but we didn't really begin to talk until we attended leadership training going into college.

October 04, 2015

As we began to talk, a connection began to form, but let's face it, neither of us knew what we were doing and were both pretty bad at flirting. Our first date was a Buffalo Bill's game that Bridget invited Dan to. While attending, a family friend asked how long we had been dating, to which Dan, as smooth as he is, blurted out "We aren't dating! We are only friends!!" Bridget still mocks Dan about this response. After a fun evening at Niagara-on-the-Lake with friends, Dan finally grew the courage to ask Bridget to make it official and they started officially "dating." Dan was so excited that he asked, her "Does this mean I can kiss you now?" to which she of course said "yes." This began a beautiful relationship.

October 17, 2015

...and onward! Through the years, we have built our relationship on love, faith, and experiences. Through the ups and downs, travels and quarantines, we have worked together to build the best relationship we can. From the Adirondacks to Ireland, Hawaii, and more, we have tried to live every day like it's our last. We are thankful for all of the amazing memories we have been able to make with our friends from around the world.

December 26, 2020

After an amazing Christmas light show in NYC, Dan suggested that Bridget watch a movie "trailer" (https://bit.ly/39bVlIM) to see if they may want to watch this cool new movie, but little did she know it was actually a video asking her to marry him!

September 04, 2022

The next step is marriage! As we prepare for the next chapter in our lives and relationship, we want to thank our family, friends, and all those who share memories and experiences with us. We would not be who we are and our relationship would not be what it is without the love, friendship, and memories that we shared. We can't wait to see you there on September 4ᵀᵸ, 2022!
