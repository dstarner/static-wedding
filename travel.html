Things to Do

As for what to do while you are visiting, we hope you stay an extra day or two to explore some of the following:

Niagara Falls State Park

332 Prospect St, Niagara Falls, NY 14303, USA
(716) 278-1794

See the mighty Niagara Falls up close! They are incredible to see, but we encourage guests to see the falls from either Luna Island [US] or the Niagara Falls, Ontario [Canada] side, if possible.


There is also the Maid of the Mist and the Niagara Gorge Trail with the Devil's Hole, the latter being one of Dan's favorites hikes!

Website

Letchworth State Park

1 Letchworth State Park, Castile, NY 14427, USA
(585) 493-3600

Letchworth State Park which is one of the most awe-inspiring parks on the East Coast. The park boasts dozens of different waterfalls, 66 miles of hiking, and some beautiful backdrops of nature. If you have some extra time in the area, we highly encourage you to check this out!

Website

Canalside

44 Prime St, Buffalo, NY 14202, USA
(716) 436-7100

Check out Canalside , Silo City, or Riverworks in Buffalo, for some food, drink, or music amongst some of Buffalo's historical buildings and waterfront.

Website

Eternal Flame Falls

Eternal Flame Hiking Trail, Orchard Park, NY 14127, USA
(716) 858-8355

The Eternal Flame hiking trail boasts a natural gas Flame behind a nice forest-surrounded waterfall. It also has Chestnut Ridge Park across the street which has some really beautiful views and a very good disc golf course.
