The Timberlodge at Arrowhead
12292 Clarence Center Rd., Akron, NY, 14001

Timberlodge at Arrowhead

Located in the picturesque village of Akron, New York is our beautiful wedding venue. The rustic and wooden look of the Timberlodge brings some of the Adirondacks-feel to Western New York and our wedding. While we might not be getting married in the mountains, we can experience our day in a beautiful building that might as well be from Saranac Lake!


With room for outdoors games, a dance floor, and a large banquet hall, we will make sure that all of our wedding guests will have a wonderful time the entire evening!
